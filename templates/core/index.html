ABOUT US

The lab focuses on creating robots that enhance social interaction and support human well-being through empathetic and inclusive design. Research at the social robotics lab focuses on various aspects of human-robot interaction, including how robots can be designed to understand and respond to human emotions, promote social inclusion, and address the ethical implications of robotic technology. technological boundaries but also ensure that technology serves society in meaningful, responsible ways.
